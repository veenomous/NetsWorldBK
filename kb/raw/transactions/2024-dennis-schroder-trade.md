## Key Takeaways
- Nets traded Dennis Schroder and a second-round pick to the Warriors for De'Anthony Melton (injured) and three second-round picks
- Continued the pattern of flipping veterans for future assets during the 2024-25 tank season
- Schroder was averaging 18.4 PPG as the Nets' leading scorer at the time of the trade

## Notes
This trade signaled to the locker room that the 2024-25 season was about development and draft positioning, not competing. Schroder was the most productive player on the roster and moving him for an injured player plus seconds made the direction clear.
