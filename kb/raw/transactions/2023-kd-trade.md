## Key Takeaways
- Nets traded Kevin Durant to the Phoenix Suns on February 9, 2023
- The return was massive: Mikal Bridges, Cam Johnson, Jalen Smith, four first-round picks (2023 unprotected, 2025 unprotected, 2027 unprotected, 2029 unprotected), and a 2028 pick swap
- This trade, combined with the Kyrie Irving trade to Dallas, marked the official start of the Nets rebuild
- The four unprotected Suns picks became the cornerstone of the rebuild asset chest

## Notes
The KD trade was widely viewed as one of the best returns for a disgruntled superstar in NBA history. The Suns gave up their entire future — four unprotected firsts from a team that would age rapidly.

Mikal Bridges and Cam Johnson gave the Nets immediate rotation talent while the picks provided long-term upside. The combination of getting both players AND picks was unusual for a star trade.
