## Key Takeaways
- On June 26, 2024, the Nets and Rockets completed a draft asset exchange
- Nets received: their own 2026 first-round pick back + Houston relinquished 2025 BKN swap right
- Nets sent: 2025 PHX swap right, 2027 PHX first, 2029 first-round pick, 2029 swap right to Houston
- This trade restructured the Nets' pick portfolio — they gave up some Suns/future assets to get their own 2026 pick back

## Notes
This trade reduced the Nets' Suns pick haul but gave them back their own 2026 first-round pick, which is critical for the rebuild — they need their own lottery picks during the tank years. Removing Houston's 2025 swap right also protected the Nets' #8 pick from being swapped away.

The 2027 PHX first that went to Houston was originally part of the KD trade return. This means the KD trade tree's total output is slightly reduced — one of the four original Suns firsts has been redirected to Houston.
