## Key Takeaways
- Memphis is in full rebuild mode after trading Jaren Jackson Jr. and Desmond Bane
- Grizzlies actively shopping Ja Morant
- Sacramento Kings asked Memphis to ADD picks to sweeten a Morant deal — weak market
- Trae Young precedent: went to Washington for CJ McCollum + Corey Kispert, no draft picks
- GM Zach Kleiman: "This is about organizational direction now"
- Scout: "There's going to be hesitation" but "several desperate teams" may pursue
- Nets NOT mentioned but fit the profile of a team with assets to acquire Morant
