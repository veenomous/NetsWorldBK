## Summary
The 2024-25 season is Year One of the Brooklyn Nets rebuild — a deliberate tank year aimed at securing a top draft pick in the loaded 2025 class. After trading Mikal Bridges in the offseason and Dennis Schroder at the deadline, the Nets fielded one of the youngest, least talented rosters in the NBA. The goal was never winning — it was development and draft positioning.

## Key Insights
- The Bridges trade (June 2024) set the tone: this team is not trying to compete
- The Schroder trade (December 2024) removed the last veteran scorer and cemented the tank
- Young players like Jalen Wilson, Noah Clowney, and Day'Ron Sharpe got significant minutes
- [[Cameron Johnson]] was the lone veteran presence, serving as a mentor and floor-spacer
- The Nets targeted a bottom-3 record to maximize lottery odds for the 2025 draft
- This season is the most important in a decade — the 2025 pick could define the next era

## Details

### The Tank Strategy
The front office executed a textbook tank: trade veterans for assets at the deadline, play young guys heavy minutes, and lose games. This wasn't passive — it was an active strategy to position for [[2025 NBA Draft|Cooper Flagg or a top-3 pick]].

Key deadline moves:
- Dennis Schroder to Warriors for De'Anthony Melton (injured) + 3 seconds
- Dorian Finney-Smith to Lakers for future assets
- Bojan Bogdanovic buyout (from Bridges trade)

### Development Focus
The silver lining of tanking is development time. Young players who got extended run:
- **Jalen Wilson** — Wing with defensive versatility, needs to improve shooting
- **Noah Clowney** — Athletic big with switchable defense, raw offensively
- **Day'Ron Sharpe** — Center with rebounding instincts, needs to expand range
- **Cam Thomas** — The most talented young player, volume scorer who needs to improve efficiency and playmaking

### The Cam Thomas Question
Cam Thomas is the most polarizing player on the roster. He can score 40 on any given night but his efficiency (sub-league-average TS%), shot selection, and lack of playmaking raise questions about whether he's a building block or a volume chucker. The 2024-25 season was supposed to answer this — results were mixed.

### Record and Lottery Position
The Nets finished among the worst records in the NBA, securing a top-5 lottery position. The exact pick will be determined by the May 2025 lottery.
